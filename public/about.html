Who We Are.

At Bolo Home, we provide peace of mind for homeowners who are away—whether you're managing a short-term rental from another city, on a long mission abroad, or simply stepping away for a season. We specialize in property care that goes beyond surface-level cleanings to keep your home running safely, smoothly, and exactly how you left it.

Where most services stop at tidying up, Bolo Home steps in to monitor what truly matters: water lines, electrical panels, HVAC systems, signs of pests, and overall home safety. We catch small problems before they become costly ones. Our scheduled, in-depth home checks are designed around your property’s unique needs—and tailored to give you confidence no matter how far you are.

Behind Bolo Home are two founders with a shared commitment to dependable service. Dexter Belling brings a solid foundation in construction and real estate, ensuring our inspections are grounded in real-world property knowledge. Coby Walsh, with experience in emergency medicine, the outdoor industry, and software engineering, brings a systems-thinking approach and a passion for reliability and innovation.

Together, they’ve built Bolo Home to be the partner you can trust when you're not home—combining hands-on expertise with professional reporting and proactive care. Let us be your eyes, ears, and boots on the ground—so you can focus on what’s ahead, knowing your home is in good hands.
